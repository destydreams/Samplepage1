Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my vehicle status?

Once confirmation finishes via our automated WhatsApp channels, automated updates regarding your driver are provided directly to your screen.

Are modifications allowed for itineraries?

Yes, you can request adjustments cleanly anytime up to 24 hours prior to pickup events via support personnel lines.

Are your drivers verified and professional?

Every single driver in our network undergoes rigorous background checks, holds a valid commercial driving license, and is highly experienced with local routes and highway safety guidelines to ensure a smooth, comfortable ride.

What happens if the vehicle encounters a breakdown during the journey?

Your safety is our absolute priority. In the rare event of a mechanical issue, our 24/7 support team immediately dispatches a replacement vehicle from our nearest local partner hub to get you back on your way safely.

Are toll taxes, parking fees, and driver allowances included in the price?

Yes, we believe in 100% transparency. All of our custom tour packages explicitly outline what is included (like tolls and fuel) so you never have to deal with unexpected hidden charges during your trip.

What types of vehicles do you offer for large groups?

We provide a diverse fleet to match your group size, ranging from comfortable 7-seater SUVs and luxury passenger vans to spacious, fully air-conditioned tourist mini-buses and luxury coaches.

What is your cancellation and refund policy?

You can cancel your booking completely free of charge up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the trip may incur a standard minimum processing fee.

Can I book a vehicle for an outstation multi-day road trip?

Absolutely. We specialize in round-trip and multi-day outstation tour transportation. Our team will help you calculate a transparent, all-inclusive package that covers driver allowances, toll taxes, and state permits.

What payment methods do you accept, and is a deposit required?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and net banking. To secure your booking, a small advance deposit is processed during checkout, with the remaining balance payable before or during your journey.
